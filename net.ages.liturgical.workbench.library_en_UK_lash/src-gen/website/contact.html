Thank you for your interest in and use of the Digital Chant Stand!

The Digital Chant Stand is a product of the Liturgical Arts Commission of the Greek Orthodox Archdiocese of America. The resources provided in this website are intended to enhance the worship experience of the Orthodox faithful with easily available liturgical texts and music.

At this time, we can only process messages regarding corrections and broken links on the DCS. Concerning these please email dcsinfo@goarch.org

For questions regarding the GOA Digital Chant Stand app, to report a bug, or to suggest an enhancement, email dcssupport@goarch.org
