Privacy Policy

AGES Initiatives values you and your privacy. We want you to feel comfortable using our apps. Listed below is the information that our apps might send out while you are using them.

1. We use Apple's analytics service to track usage of our iOS apps. This records interesting stuff like the number of people who are using the app and what version of iOS they're running. No personably identifiable information is sent. You can opt in or out of this service by opening the iOS Settings app and navigating to Privacy/Analytics.

2. If you have provided us with your email address, this will only used for occasional emails of importance about AGES Initiatives and our apps. We will not give your email address to anyone else or use it for other purposes.
